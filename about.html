About North Angle

Learn about our philosophy, team, and approach to architecture

Baidar Hussain

Principal Architect & Founder

With over 15 years of experience in architectural design, Baidar Hussain founded North Angle with a vision to create spaces that seamlessly blend functionality with aesthetic appeal. His work has been recognized with multiple awards for innovation and sustainability.

Baidar's approach to architecture is deeply influenced by his travels and studies of both traditional and contemporary design principles. He believes that good design should not only serve its purpose but also elevate the human experience.

Credentials

  • Master of Architecture, Harvard University
  • Licensed Architect in 5 states
  • LEED Accredited Professional
  • AIA Member since 2010

The North Angle Story

2008

Foundation

North Angle was founded with a focus on residential architecture, completing its first project - a modern family home in the suburbs.

2012

Expansion

The firm expanded into commercial architecture, designing office spaces that prioritize employee wellbeing and productivity.

2016

Sustainability Focus

North Angle became a leader in sustainable design, incorporating green technologies and passive design principles into all projects.

2020

International Recognition

The firm received international acclaim for the Riverfront Cultural Center, establishing North Angle as a global design practice.

Our Design Philosophy

At North Angle, we approach each project with three core principles:

Contextual Harmony

Our designs respond to their environment, creating a dialogue between built form and natural surroundings.

Human-Centered Design

We prioritize the experience of the people who will inhabit our spaces, ensuring comfort, functionality, and inspiration.

Sustainable Future

Every project incorporates sustainable practices, reducing environmental impact while creating healthier spaces.
